About Us

Victorious audio is an audio company that specializes in economical audio-related products with the main goal of producing low-cost high-performance products that are viable for the largest number of customers. The business is a sole proprietorship with admissions to grow into a partnership and a corporation. It will be run as a part-time venture by the owner. The idea for low-cost guitar pedals is made possible by the RP-2040 micro-processor and 3D printing, as the RP-2040 is low-cost and outperforms every other microprocessor by at least twofold in the same price bracket. 3d printing allows for rapid development and small manufacturing of mechanical components at a price and rate that is unmatched by any other process. Victorious audio will focus on producing MIDI-Controllers for guitarists and musicians.
